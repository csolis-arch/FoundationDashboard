Methodology: FMV projections apply 7.7% gross annual return to prior year-end FMV, then subtract the full RMD. RMD = 5.0% × prior year-end FMV per IRC §4942 — assumed met in full each year so no carryforward accumulates. Admin = 0.70% × prior year-end FMV (2024 990 rate: $1,751,963 ÷ $251,883,734 = 0.696%). Available for Grants = RMD minus Admin. Net FMV growth = 7.7% − 5.0% = 2.7% per year; both FMV and available grants compound at this rate. 2024 actual note: The 2024 990 shows qualifying distributions of $7.23M against an $11.69M requirement, a $4.46M shortfall carried to 2025. This table assumes full compliance from 2025 onward. All figures are estimates; actual results will vary with market conditions and investment decisions.
