Consultant Observations
🔵
Recommendation: Build a pledge reserve policy. Best practice for foundations with active multi-year pledges is to set aside a "pledge reserve" — often 1–2× the next year's obligations — in a designated fund or investment sleeve. This prevents investment volatility from forcing a pledge default.

Dependency Risk Insights
Recommendations
🔴
Adopt a dependency policy. Best practice: no single grantee should receive more than 20–30% of their total operating budget from one foundation. For any org where this is suspected, request a copy of their most recent Form 990 or audited financials before renewing.
🟠
Stage exit strategies for high-dependency orgs. If the Foundation ever needs to reduce or exit a grant, high-dependency orgs need 12–18 months notice and transition support — not just a grant decline letter. Consider adding this language to grant agreements.
🔵
Consider capacity-building supplements. For orgs rated High or Medium risk, a supplemental $5–10K grant earmarked for "fundraising infrastructure" is a high-leverage intervention that reduces dependency over time.
